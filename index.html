Meet DJ Jason Brain

With hundreds of events and 25+ years of experience, DJ Jason Brain is one of the most recognized wedding DJs in Central Ontario.

Our team caters to the mood and taste of your event perfectly with our extensive variety, genre, and generations of music. We go well beyond just music! We offer Karaoke, Music Video Road Shows, Special Effects, and Lighting to literally bring your party to life.

From the 40's to the Top 40's, we have your music covered.

Reputation is everything! The vast majority of our clients are referred to us by previous customers, banquet halls, and guests that have attended one of our events. Our reasonable prices, outstanding service, and enormous selection of music have made AVDJ one of the most widely respected and referred DJ services in Ontario.

Our Services

AVDJ is ready when you need us! Professional equipment, licensed music, and full liability insurance. Proud member of the CDJA (Canadian Disc Jockey Association).

Weddings

The single most important day of your life. We believe your wedding could be the most important day of your life, and one of the most stressful. When it comes to the wedding DJ, we want you to relax and place your trust in an experienced and professional company. We consult with all couples prior to the wedding day discussing formats, music tastes, and special dances.

Corporate Events

No matter what the reason, a company party is a great way to bring employees together. AVDJ is available any day of the week to provide our professional services for your holiday party, awards night, training sessions, or team building events. Add Karaoke for extra fun!

Schools & Organizations

We have the experience to entertain any crowd, including schools, and we have the professionalism to screen all our music to ensure NO FOUL LANGUAGE is played. We are both licensed and insured and can provide Police Background Checks on staff when required.

Special Events

From new store openings, product promotions, car shows, to wedding and fashion shows, AVDJ is there to provide the sound equipment, music, and even host the event on your behalf.

Karaoke

With a wide variety of songs and our professional sound equipment, a night of karaoke becomes very entertaining. Mixed in are sets of dance music to complete the fun.

Lighting & Special Effects

No matter what your needs, AVDJ can accommodate from LED lighting to foggers, we have the equipment to make it happen.
